He shifts a great distance towards the front rapidly. A moment later, he moves considerably to the left. In the second right before, the right knee and his left knee are unbent. From this position, the right knee bends and not long after, it extends and rapidly. Meanwhile, the left foot moves farther from the right one quickly and immediately after, moves nearer to the right one rapidly. Immediately after, the left foot moves farther from the right one rapidly, and simultaneously, it moves nearer to the right one briskly.
