He moves far to the left, and not long after, shifts far towards the back. Not too long before, his left foot moves nearer to the right one briskly, and shortly after, moves nearer to the right one speedily. Just moments before, the right foot moves farther from the left one, and a moment later, spreads away from the left one. Not long after, he shifts far to the right. A second later, the right foot gets closer to the left one. A few seconds later, it moves nearer to the left one. A few seconds earlier, the left foot moves farther from the right one and a few seconds later, it moves farther from the right one. After some time, he moves far over to the left and simultaneously, leans slightly into his right. Right after, he shifts far over to the front.
