She shifts a great distance to the front speedily. Not long after, she shifts downwards. A second later, her right foot spreads away from the left one. Right after, the right foot is to the front, spread far apart from the left foot and from that pose, her left foot gets closer to the right one. Immediately after, the left foot spreads away from the right one quickly. Immediately after, it moves farther from the right one. Right before, the left foot moves nearer to the right one briskly. Shortly after, the left foot moves nearer to the right one.
