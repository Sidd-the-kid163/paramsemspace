His left knee is joined with the right and with that pose, his right knee spreads away from the left one and not long after, moves nearer to the left one. Not too long before, he shifts to the left and a moment later, he shifts far to the right. After a while, he shifts considerably to the left. Not too long before, his right knee is right next to the other, and from that pose, the left knee moves farther from the right one, and a moment later, it moves nearer to the right one.
