She moves to the front, and simultaneously, shifts far over to the left rapidly, and after a while, shifts towards the front. Shortly after, her left foot moves nearer to the right one. A second later, the left foot spreads significantly apart from the right one and a moment later, moves nearer to the right one. A second later, her left foot spreads away from the right one. Shortly after, she shifts a great distance to the right briskly. In the meantime, her feet are spread apart. From this position, the left foot gets closer to the right one and right after, it moves farther from the right one, and a moment later, her left foot moves nearer to the right one briskly.
