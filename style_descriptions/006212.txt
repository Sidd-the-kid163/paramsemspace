He shifts far over to the front rapidly. In the meantime, his legs are right next to each other and from this position, the right foot moves farther from the left one. Shortly after, it gets closer to the left one. In the second right before, his left foot spreads away from the right one. In the meantime, it moves nearer to the right one. Shortly after, his right foot spreads away from the left one. Right after, the left foot moves nearer to the right one, and right after, moves farther from the right one rapidly, and not long after, it gets closer to the right one. Meanwhile, the left hand gets closer to the left knee.
