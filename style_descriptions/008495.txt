He moves far over to the front. A moment later, his right hand moves farther from his right knee. Right after, his left hand spreads away from his left knee and after a while, it moves nearer to the left knee. Not too long before, the right knee is unbent. From this stance, his right knee bends. Right after, it extends. A second later, the left knee is straight and from this stance, the left knee bends. Immediately after, it extends and speedily. In the meantime, his right hand moves nearer to the right knee.
