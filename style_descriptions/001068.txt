She moves a great distance to the left. Right after, the right knee is unbent, and from that pose, her right knee bends and rapidly, and right after, it extends. Right before, both hands move farther from the knees speedily and a second later, they move nearer to her knees briskly. In the second right before, her right hand spreads away from her left knee. Right after, it gets closer to the left knee briskly.
