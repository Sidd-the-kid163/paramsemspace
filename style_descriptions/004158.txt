She moves far to the front quickly. Not long after, both knees are straight, and from that pose, the left knee bends and quickly. Immediately after, it extends. In the meantime, her left foot spreads away from the right one. Immediately after, the right knee is straight, and then, her right knee bends and speedily and a second later, extends and briskly. In the second right before, the right foot gets closer to the left one. A second later, the left foot moves farther from the right one briskly and immediately after, it moves nearer to the right one. Right after, her left foot moves farther from the right one and immediately after, it moves nearer to the right one. Right after, the left foot spreads away from the right one and not long after, it moves nearer to the right one. In the second right before, her left knee is straight. Then, her left knee bends.
