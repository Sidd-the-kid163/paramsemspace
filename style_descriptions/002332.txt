He moves a great distance towards the front extremely fast, and a second later, moves upwards. In the second right before, the left foot moves farther from the right one speedily. Immediately after, the left foot is spread far apart from his right foot. It is in front. Then, the left foot moves nearer to the right one speedily, and meanwhile, it moves farther from the right one rapidly. Right after, the left foot moves nearer to the right one rapidly. A few seconds later, it moves farther from the right one briskly. Just before, his left knee is unbent. With that pose, his left knee bends and speedily and immediately after, extends and rapidly. Right after, his right foot moves nearer to the left one.
