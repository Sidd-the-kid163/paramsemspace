He moves far over towards the front. Eventually, he shifts a great distance to the right. Just before, his left knee is unbent, his right knee is nearly bent. Then, his knees bend. A few seconds later, his right knee is bent, and with that pose, his right knee extends completely and briskly. Not long after, his left foot gets closer to the right one, and immediately after, it moves farther from the right one. Soon after, it spreads away from the right one. Not too long before, the left knee is in l-shape. Then, his left knee extends. A moment later, the right knee is unbent, and from this position, the right knee bends. A second later, it extends. Simultaneously, the right foot gets closer to the left one. Shortly after, his left foot gets closer to the right one. Immediately after, it spreads away from the right one. Simultaneously, he leans into his right. After a while, the right knee is straight. From that pose, his right knee bends.
