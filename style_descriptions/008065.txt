He shifts considerably towards the front. Soon after, he shifts to the back. Right before, the left knee and his right knee are unbent. From this stance, the left knee extends and very rapidly. In the meantime, the left foot spreads away from the right one very fast. A second later, it moves nearer to the right one quickly. Right before, his left hand moves farther from his left knee. A second later, it moves nearer to his left knee. Meanwhile, his left knee is unbent, and from that pose, his left knee bends. Immediately after, his left knee is in l-shape and from this position, his left knee extends.
